Project Based Learning

How will people around the world be affected by rising sea levels due to global climate change?

This is a project that is for upper level high school students in history or social studies class. This class project is made in connection with the project Community Climate Action. Both of these projects are made for Moreland University teacher certification program.

Learning objectives

Students will work in groups of four

Students will learn about the geoprahic regions that will be affected by the rising sea levels. Within these regions the students will choose a group of people that calls this region their home. The group will then learn about the history of this group. In addition to this, they will learn about what is being done to help these populations to prepare for the incoming difficulties. By the end of this project the groups will create a social media campaign that will inform the public about these groups of people and how they can be helped.

Twenty-First Century Skills

Communication

Students will need to work as a group to create their final social media campaign. Furthermore, they will need to talk about how they will do the research and divide the work. Finally, The students will need to create a message for their campaigns.

Critical Thinking

Students will need to come up with ideas on how to impliment the social media campaign. For example, what platforms will reach which audience or how can we best helpe these people in need. Students will need to be able to explain how their ideas to help the populations they chose.

Technology and Media

Students will need to decide which apps or platforms they will use to create their media campaign. Students will be able to choose whether they want to make YouTube or TikTok videos. Or will they create a campaign around using AR apps and geotagging. Students will get many opportunites to use a variety of technology in this project.

Creativiy

The design of their media is completely up to the students to design. They will need to create something attention grabbing so that the general public will want to watch or look at the materials they make.

Science

Students will be learing about climate science and how quickly the sea levels are rising. They might also need to learn about zoological and ecological science that would be connected to the food or fresh water sources that the people might need to survive.

Technology

Students will learn a lot about social media and internet based technology in order to complete their social media campaign. They will need to relay on internet based sources, like google maps, to help them learn about the geography of where the people live or be relocated to.

Engineering

Students will learn about some of the civil engineering feats that are being used to delay to flooding of cities. This could be in the forms of sea walls canals to divert water and many other possible solutions.

Art

Use of graphic disign or video direction will be key skills to complete thier final project in this PBL. The social medica campaign needs to be visually appealing so that the audience will want to pay attention. The students will need to learn about what makes different ad campaigns attractive. The various choices the students can make for creating their social media campaign can allow students to demonstrate, learn, and grow in artistic expression.

Math

How many people will need to be relocated? How much land will be covered by water? How much money will these projects cost? While the math needed to calculate these is not complex, it will bring some need for calculation to talk about the affects of the people and their lively hoods.

How are students evaluated?

Students will be evaluated by their teacher and by their peers. Click on this link to see the rubric used for this project. Click Here

Peer Review

Students will be given a copy of the rubric and another groups project. Students will check the project compared to the rubric. Afterwards, they will need to write notes to the original group about what they thought was done well, and what they thing needs to be improved upon. This peer review is part of their final grade for this project as well.

Teacher Review

Using the rubric the teacher will grade their project. Additionally, the teacher will be paying attention to how well the students work together, and the quality of the final media campaign produced. The teacher will also provide guidence throughout the project giving feedback when necessary. After the research stage has finished, teacher will have students give an update to the teacher, which will be followed by teacher feedback and suggestions. As these are high school students, the teacher wants to remain as hands off as possible when monitoring students progress.

Completed Project

The students final social media campaign will be shared with their classmate and the school. The teacher and their peers will provide possitive and constructive criticism of the project. While working on the project, the teacher will provide suggestions if the students get stuck or are unsure how to use certain social media technologies. If needed, the teacher can provide links to YouTube videos that provide extra tutorials on how to build infographics, edit video, make AR geotags and so on.
